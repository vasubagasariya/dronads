Unipole Advertising

Unipoles are large-format outdoor advertising structures supported by a single pole, typically installed along highways, main roads, flyovers, and entry points of cities. Due to their height and size, they are visible from long distances, making them highly effective for brand awareness campaigns.

At Dron Ads, we provide premium unipole placements that ensure your brand is seen by thousands of commuters every day, building trust and credibility in prominent locations.

  • Large-format displays visible from 500+ meters
  • Premium positions along NH, SH & flyovers
  • Both front-lit and backlit options available
  • Steel structure with weather-resistant printing
  • Complete installation and maintenance support
