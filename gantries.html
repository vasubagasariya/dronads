Gantry Advertising

Gantries are large advertising structures installed across the width of a road, supported by pillars on both sides. Unlike side-mounted hoardings, gantries face traffic head-on, ensuring your message is seen clearly by drivers, riders, and passengers.

At Dron Ads, we deliver well-planned gantry advertising solutions that maximize reach, visibility, and brand recall across roads, flyovers, and highways.

  • Spans entire road width for unavoidable visibility
  • Direct line-of-sight to all oncoming traffic
  • Premium locations on flyovers, bridges & highways
  • Government-approved structures with full compliance
  • Ideal for high-frequency mass-reach campaigns

Key Advantages

Why Choose Gantry Advertising

Direct Line-of-Sight

Placed right in front of moving traffic, gantries are impossible to ignore and highly effective for instant communication.

High Recall Value

Repeated exposure during daily commutes helps create strong brand recall and long-lasting impressions.

24x7 Brand Exposure

Gantries provide round-the-clock visibility, delivering consistent brand presence without digital reliance.

Builds Brand Authority

Advertising on gantries is perceived as premium, established, and credible due to the scale of the medium.
