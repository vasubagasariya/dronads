BQS Advertising

Bus Queue Shelter (BQS) advertising involves placing brand messages on the panels of bus stops. These shelters are located at high-traffic areas, ensuring your ad is seen by commuters, pedestrians, and motorists waiting or passing by.

At Dron Ads, we offer strategically placed BQS advertising that delivers high visibility and consistent brand recall across urban and semi-urban locations.

  • High dwell-time advertising at bus stops
  • Strategic locations at major transit points
  • Both illuminated and non-illuminated options
  • Reaches daily commuters and pedestrians
  • Ideal for brand awareness and retail promotions

Key Advantages

Why Choose BQS Advertising

High Dwell Time

Commuters waiting for buses spend considerable time at shelters, leading to deeper engagement with your brand message.

Constant Visibility

BQS ads are visible 24x7 to everyone on the road, including pedestrians and drivers, creating thousands of daily impressions.

Hyper-Local Targeting

Select specific bus stops near your business or target audience to drive local traffic and awareness effectively.

Affordable Branding

BQS offers an economical way to achieve high reach and frequency compared to larger outdoor advertising formats.
